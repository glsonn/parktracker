Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 12, 2026

The State Parks Passport ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any information you provide while using the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed to collect minimal information. We may collect:

  • Anonymous User Identifier: A randomly generated UUID stored in your browser’s local storage to distinguish your visits.
  • Visit Data: Information about parks you mark as visited, including visit dates and optional notes.
  • Usage Data: Basic, anonymized analytics data (if implemented) to understand how the App is used.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the App’s functionality.
  • Track your progress and achievements.
  • Improve the App’s performance and user experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

Visit data is stored securely using Supabase. While reasonable measures are taken to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

4. No Personal Information Required

The App does not require you to create an account or provide personally identifiable information such as your name or email address.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

The App uses browser local storage to save your anonymous user identifier and improve your experience. This data remains on your device unless you clear your browser storage.

6. Third-Party Services

The App relies on third-party services, including Supabase for data storage and Vercel for hosting. These services may collect limited technical information necessary for their operation.

7. Your Choices

You can:

  • Clear your browser’s local storage to remove your identifier and associated data.
  • Stop using the App at any time.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
